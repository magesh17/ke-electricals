Company overview

We would like to introduce ourselves as one of the leading manufactures of "Current Transformers" brand name "KE". We have been in this field manufacturing various type of current transformers (CT) like Metering CT, Protection CT, Rectangular type CT, Core Balance CT, WPL CT, Resin Cast CT, Special purpose CT and Summation CT.

We are supplying to industries, Government, non Government Organizations, Instituions, TNEB, Electrical Contractors, Switch gear manufactures, Electrical Panel Board Manufactures and Industrial Electrical dealers across India.

We are proud to say that we are the TNEB approved supplier and CPRI certified current transformer manufacturer. We have equipped with good qualified and experienced technical personals. We can undertake any type of current transformers, our product is made of quality material and prices are very competitive.
